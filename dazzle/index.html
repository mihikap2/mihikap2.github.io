Otto's Mission

The advent of technology has simplified countless processes over the past century. However, the concept of the timecard has been left behind.
Until now.

MEET OTTO

Otto is rooted in human behavioral research as opposed to the antiquated focus of capturing billable time as a manual/functional process.

Use on the Go

Otto is the timecard building digital assistant that captures the full extent of your billable hours by simply having a conversation with you. Whether you’re on the way home from work or riding down the elevator from a client meeting, Otto is accessible wherever you are, step for step.

Never Miss a Beat

Notifications are tailormade for when it’s convenient for you. With Otto, you’ll never have to carve out time in your calendar for billing again.

Otto does the Heavy Lifting

We recognize that speaking with a coworker about what you just accomplished seems easier than creating a narrative for a timecard – even though they cover the same topics. Talk to Otto about the task you just completed or the meeting you just finished, and he will create the timecard for you.

Knows Your Schedule

Gone are the days of flipping back and forth from your calendar to your timecard. Otto uses your schedule to learn who and what matters you. Optimize your workflow so you have more time, less stress, and can focus on the important things in your day.

Streamlined Timecard Creation

Otto standardizes your timecards, leading to cleaner, more consistent billings that provide simplicity for you, your team, and your client.
