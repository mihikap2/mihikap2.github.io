Used Pandas to analyze a dataset published by the University of Illinois at Urbana Champaign(UIUC) showing Enrollment by Curriculum, Race, Sex, Residency, to find the Top 10 majors with the most Gender Disparity, and the most Male and Female Dominated majors, from among majors with at least 50 students. Used the D3.js library in JavaScript, along with HTML and CSS, to create an interactive bar graph showing the percentage of Gender Disparity in these majors.
